Begin by creating a folder.

Click the green button at the bottom of the page to add a folder.

Each folder displays how many words are contained within them and how many words are currently reviewable.

Add words to a folder.

Use the "ADD WORD" button at the top to add a new word to the folder.

Click on a word to see more information or to update it.

Make a word reviewable.

A progress bar will appear beside the word, showing the words current level

MyBrary will test you on this word periodically. The level of the word will increase everytime you get it right, and so will the review cooldown duration.

Review your words.

When words are ready for review, a notification card will be displayed at the bottom with the number of current reviews available.

Clicking on this button will begin the review.

Tap on the card to flip it and see the answer. Swipe left if you got it wrong. Swipe right if you were right.

The level and cooldown of the word will be adjusted accordingly.

Reach level 20 to master the word!
